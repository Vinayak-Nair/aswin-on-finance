Do I need to file an Income Tax Return (ITR) in India if I only have small interest/rent income?

Filing is generally required if India-taxable income exceeds the exemption limit. Even if below, you may file to claim TDS refunds.

  • To carry forward losses
  • Simply to keep a financial record

Pro tip: Filing a nil return won't hurt and can only help in various ways (refunds, compliance).

It's a straightforward process you can do online (the Income Tax department's e-filing portal allows NRIs to file electronically from abroad). The due date is usually July 31st.

If you ever erroneously paid any advance tax or TDS, filing is the only way to reconcile that. Evaluate your situation: if truly trivial income with no tax at stake, it's your call; if any money is locked in TDS, go ahead and file.
