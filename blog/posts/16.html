Can I still claim 80C deductions in India as an NRI?

Yes, NRIs can generally claim eligible Chapter VIA deductions like 80C against India-taxable income, subject to instrument eligibility and proof.

Key Point: 80C helps only against India-taxable income (rent, capital gains, etc.). If you have no taxable income in India, 80C doesn't help you.
