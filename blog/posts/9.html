I have rental income from India ‚Äì how is it taxed and what about TDS?

Rent paid to NRIs is typically subject to TDS under section 195 (tenant deducts and deposits). You file ITR to claim credit/refund and compute actual tax.

üè† How It Works

  • Your tenant is legally required to deduct TDS before paying you rent
  • The TDS rate for NRI rental income is typically around 30% (plus surcharge and cess)
  • This is much higher than the 10% for residents

Why so high? Since NRIs are abroad, the government wants to ensure tax is collected upfront.

üìã Tenant Obligations

Your tenant must:

  1. Deduct TDS from rent payments
  2. Deposit TDS on time to the government
  3. File quarterly TDS returns
  4. Issue Form 16A to you

If TDS credit doesn't reflect in your Form 26AS, it can block refunds ‚Äì follow up early with your tenant.

üí∞ Getting Refunds

Often, the TDS deducted exceeds your actual tax liability. When you file your ITR:

  • Compute your actual tax on rental income
  • Claim credit for TDS paid
  • Get refund of excess TDS
