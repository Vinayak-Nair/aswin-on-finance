I'm an NRI and totally confused about how to invest in Indian stocks. How are people doing this?

Investing in Indian stocks as a UAE-based NRI is possible, but it requires a special account setup. When you move abroad, you can't use your old resident trading account (like a standard Zerodha or Groww account) without modifications. In fact, platforms such as Groww currently do not support NRI investments at all. This means you'll need to shift to a broker that offers NRI services.
