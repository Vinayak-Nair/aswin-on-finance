Find your residential status for FY 2024-25

NRI — Non-Resident Indian

A person who doesn't meet the residency criteria. Only Indian income is taxed.

ROR — Resident and Ordinarily Resident

A person who qualifies as resident and has been in India regularly. Global income is taxed.

RNOR — Resident but Not Ordinarily Resident

A transitional status between NRI and ROR. Indian income is taxed, foreign income generally isn't.
